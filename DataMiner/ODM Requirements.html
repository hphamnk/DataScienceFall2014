cs378 Data Science Data Mining Project

Convince me that you have found something very interesting in your data or demonstrate a valiant attempt.

This is a difficult project and grading will be harder than previous projects. I strongly recommend going through Lessons 7, 8, and 9 in the Oracle Data Mining Activity Guide the way we did with Lessons 5, and 6 in class on Oct. 30th. This should only take an hour or two. Then, start applying all of the techniques learned in Lessons 5 - 9 to your data sets along with doing appropriate visualizations in Tableau. This should be done sooner than later - just a warning.

A Professional Quality Project Presentation is part of convincing me that you have found something very interesting in your data. The elements of such a presentation should be to tell me

  • the data you analyzed
  • what you found that's very interesting
  • how the data supports what you found
  • the technology you used in your findings via a short, well thought-out demo
  • what additional follow-up investigations could be done

This should be done in a simple, concise, straight-forward manner in the time allotted either using power point or a KnitR doc (which  will be required for the final GiThub-based project submission). It would be wise to rehearse your presentation several times before your scheduled presentation time.
